My Favourite Most Awaited Films in 2025-26Trailers

1.Peddi

The film's official title was announced as Peddi on 27 March 2025, coinciding with         Ram Charan's 40th birthday. To mark the occasion, the first-look posters were released. The first poster presents Ram Charan in a rugged appearance with messy hair, an unkempt beard, and a nose ring, wearing a red-striped outfit while smoking a cigar. The second poster depicts him holding an old cricket bat in a rustic village stadium under floodlights, suggesting the film’s sports-centric theme.

2.OG

OG is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action crime film written and directed by Sujeeth, and produced by D. V. V. Danayya under the banner of DVV Entertainment. The film stars Pawan Kalyan in the titular role, Emraan Hashmi (in his Telugu film debut) and Priyanka Mohan. The film was officially announced in December 2022, and principal photography commenced in April 2023 in Mumbai. The music is composed by Thaman S, while cinematography is handled by Ravi K. Chandran and Manoj Paramahamsa, and editing by Naveen Nooli. OG is scheduled for release on 25 September 2025.

3.Mirai

Mirai - Super Yodha is a Telugu fantasy action adventure movie directed by Karthik Gattamneni. The movie casts Teja Sajja and Ritika Nayak in the main lead roles. The music was composed by Gowra Hari while the film is produced by TG Vishwa Prasad under People Media Factory banner. Following his success in the blockbuster movie HanuMan, Teja Sajja is reprising the role of a superhero in Mirai - Super Yodha. According to industry sources, Manchu Manoj Kumar is set to portray the main antagonist in Mirai.
